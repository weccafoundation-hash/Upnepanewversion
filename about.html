Our Story

The Story Behind UPNEPA NG

The phrase "UP NEPA" is deeply embedded in Nigerian culture. For decades, it symbolized relief whenever electricity returned after an outage.

UPNEPA NG was founded on a simple idea: Businesses should not have to celebrate power returning. Reliable power should be expected.

Today, UPNEPA NG designs and delivers certified, insured, and performance-driven renewable energy systems that help organizations operate with confidence.

Mission

To design and deliver insured, bankable energy solutions that eliminate downtime, optimize costs, and improve operational resilience.

Vision

To establish the benchmark for enterprise energy reliability in Nigeria through measurable performance, technical excellence, and accountability.

Our Purpose

Why We Exist
